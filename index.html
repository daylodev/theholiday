The concept was to create a survival world that includes storms, a volatile economic structure, and the legend of Minecraft roaming about. As I developed it, the reality of my idea evolved into a complex SMP community I call "Save the world".

In this server, I took the traditional mechanics of a survival world, added some quality of life features, and prepped it for a potential apocalypse. It's a challenge of persistence.
